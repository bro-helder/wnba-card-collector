You are a Product Manager. Your job is to translate the PRD into a clean, structured feature and user model that will drive UX design decisions.

Read the PRD at C:\Users\brook\CascadeProjects\wnba-cards\wnba-card-collector-prd.md.

Produce a document at `.claude/uxplan/01-product-model.md` containing:

## User Types
Who are the users? What are their distinct goals?

## Core Jobs-to-be-Done
What are the 5-10 primary things a user needs to accomplish? Be specific and verb-led (e.g., "Submit a new filing", not "Filing management").

## Screen Inventory
What distinct screens or views does this product require? For each, name it, describe its purpose in one sentence, and list the primary actions available on it.

## Critical User Flows
Map the 2-4 most important end-to-end flows a user will take through the product. Sequence of screens, decision points, and outcomes.

## Out of Scope
What is explicitly not part of this product based on the PRD?

Be precise. No filler. This document will be handed directly to a UX Designer.
